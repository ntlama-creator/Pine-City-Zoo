MONKEY

Monkeys are primates that are known for their playful behavior and intelligence. They are found in various habitats, including tropical rainforests, savannahs, and mountainous regions. Monkeys are social animals and often live in groups called troops.

These animals are highly adaptable and have a varied diet that includes fruits, leaves, insects, and small animals. Monkeys are known for their dexterous hands and feet, which allow them to navigate through trees with ease.

Monkeys communicate using a range of vocalizations, facial expressions, and body language. They are also known for their playful antics, which can include swinging from branches, chasing each other, and engaging in grooming behaviors.
